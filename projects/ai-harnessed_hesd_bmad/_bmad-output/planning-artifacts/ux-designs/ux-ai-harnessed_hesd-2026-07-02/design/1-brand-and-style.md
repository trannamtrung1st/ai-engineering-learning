# Brand & Style

HESD Attendance is an **internal workshop tool** — not a consumer habit app, not enterprise SaaS chrome. The visual posture is **Neobrutalism**: flat solids, **2px black outlines**, **hard offset shadows**, **0px corners**. It reads clearly on a **projector** from the back of a room and on a **phone** held at arm's length.

The brand promise is **honest validation**. GPS checks, roster checks, and token expiry are **features**, not bugs. Visual language supports that: status colors are unambiguous, errors are loud but actionable, and the projected QR surface is the hero — everything else steps back.

Inherits token definitions from `docs/ui-ux/design-system/`. This DESIGN spine is the product-layer contract; module files are the implementation reference. Agents route through `.agents/skills/neobrutalism-design-system` before building UI.
