# Responsive & Platform

| Surface | Breakpoint behavior |
|---|---|
| Student mobile | Mobile-first; single column; safe-area padding for notched phones |
| Instructor / Admin | ≥768px sidebar nav; <768px collapsible nav drawer |
| QR Display | Scales QR to max 70vh; typography uses `{typography.display-mobile}` minimum on small projectors |
| Browsers | Chrome + Safari iOS/Android (student); modern evergreen desktop (staff) |

Camera QR scan is **out of app** — OS camera app opens URL. In-app QR scanning is a non-goal.
