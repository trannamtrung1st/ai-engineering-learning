# Epic List

### Epic 1: Platform Foundation & Secure Access
Staff and students can sign in to role-gated surfaces on a deployed, seeded platform.
**FRs covered:** FR3, FR3a

### Epic 2: Admin Account & Roster Management
Admin can provision all workshop participants and maintain cohort rosters before sessions.
**FRs covered:** FR1, FR2, FR4, FR5, FR6

### Epic 3: Instructor Session Setup & Live QR Display
Instructor can create, configure, activate a workshop session and project a rotating QR code.
**FRs covered:** FR7, FR8, FR9, FR10

### Epic 4: Student Mobile Check-In
Students scan QR and complete validated, GPS-enforced check-in with clear Vietnamese outcomes.
**FRs covered:** FR11, FR12, FR13, FR16

### Epic 5: Instructor Live Monitoring, Overrides & Export
Instructor monitors realtime attendance, corrects exceptions, and exports final reports.
**FRs covered:** FR14, FR15, FR18

### Epic 6: Admin Audit Investigation
Admin can browse and filter the full audit trail of check-in attempts across all sessions.
**FRs covered:** FR17
