# 9. Success Metrics

**Primary**

- **SM-1:** ≥ **95%** of on-roster Students achieve successful automated check-in within **15 minutes** of session activation. Validates FR-10, FR-12, FR-13.
- **SM-2:** Instructor-reported **manual override rate < 10%** of roster per session. Validates FR-15. Counterbalances over-reliance on GPS rigidity.

**Secondary**

- **SM-3:** Admin completes cohort provisioning (accounts + roster) for 150 students in **< 30 minutes** including CSV error fixes. Validates FR-2, FR-6.
- **SM-4:** Post-session CSV export matches instructor's ground-truth spot check on **100%** of sessions in pilot. Validates FR-18.

**Counter-metrics (do not optimize)**

- **SM-C1:** Do not minimize check-in duration below validation thoroughness — sub-5-second check-in is not a goal if it weakens GPS or auth checks.
